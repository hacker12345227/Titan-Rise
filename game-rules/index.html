1. Respect Others

No harassment, bullying, or offensive language. Treat staff and players with respect.

2. Chat Conduct

No spamming, advertising, or inappropriate messages. Keep chat friendly and appropriate for all ages.

3. PvP Rules

PvP is allowed unless marked otherwise. No spawn-killing or targeting AFK players. PvP cheats are strictly forbidden.

4. Cheating & Exploits

No hacks, mods, or scripts giving unfair advantages. Exploiting bugs or glitches is forbidden. Report them to staff.

5. Griefing & Building

Do not destroy or steal from other players’ builds. Claim your land using server tools.

6. Economy & Trading

Trade fairly—no scamming or exploiting the economy. Staff may intervene in suspicious trades.

7. Item & Resource Use

Do not take items from other players without permission. Protect valuable resources; theft is punishable.

8. Commands & Admin Tools

Do not abuse server commands or admin tools. Only staff may use moderation commands.

9. Reporting Issues

Report hacks, griefing, or rule-breaking to staff. Do not take matters into your own hands.

10. Consequences

Breaking rules may result in warnings, kicks, temporary or permanent bans. Serious or repeated offenses will face stronger consequences.
