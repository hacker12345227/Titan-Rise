TitanRise - Game Rules

1. Respect Others

No harassment, bullying, or offensive language. Treat staff and players with respect.

2. Chat Conduct

No spamming or inappropriate messages. Keep chat friendly and appropriate for all ages.

3. PvP Rules

PvP is allowed everywhere unless marked otherwise. No PvP cheats are allowed.

4. Cheating & Exploits

No hacks, mods, or scripts giving unfair advantages (X-ray, fly, speed, etc.). Exploiting server bugs is forbidden; report them to staff.

5. Griefing & Building

Griefing is allowed. Destroying, raiding, and building over other players is part of the server experience.

6. Economy & Trading

Scamming is allowed. Trade and economy interactions are completely free-form.

7. Item & Resource Use

Stealing items from other players is allowed. Protect your valuables carefully!

8. Commands & Admin Tools

Do not abuse server commands or admin tools. Only staff may use moderation commands.

9. Reporting Issues

Report hacks, exploits, or other rule-breaking (like cheating) to staff. Do not take matters into your own hands.

10. Consequences

Breaking the server rules (like cheating) may result in warnings, kicks, or bans. Normal gameplay actions like griefing or scamming do not count as punishable offenses.
