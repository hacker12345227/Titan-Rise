TitanRise - Discord Rules

1. Respect & Conduct

No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks. Treat everyone politely, including staff.

2. Chat Channels

Keep channels on-topic. No spamming, excessive emojis, or irrelevant links.

3. Language

English only (unless channel allows other languages). No offensive, NSFW, or inappropriate content.

4. Advertising

No advertising other servers, products, or services without permission.

5. Voice Channels

Use push-to-talk if necessary. No excessive noise, mic spam, or trolling in voice chats.

6. Privacy & Safety

Do not share personal info of yourself or others. No doxxing or malicious content.

7. Bot Commands

Use bots responsibly; do not spam commands. Respect bot usage limits.

8. Staff Respect

Listen to moderators and admins. Staff decisions are final; do not argue publicly.

9. Reporting

Report rule violations to staff via DM or report channels. Do not harass other players publicly.

10. Consequences

Violations may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans. Repeated offenses lead to stronger punishments.
